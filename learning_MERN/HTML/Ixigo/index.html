About ixigo

ixigo is an AI-powered travel app and technology company focused on empowering Indian travellers to plan, book and manage their trips seamlessly. ixigo provides a one-stop platform for all travel needs, including flights, hotels, trains, buses, and cabs, at the best prices. Founded in 2006 and headquartered in Gurugram, India, ixigo has grown to be one of India's most popular and trusted travel apps, serving over 30 crore users across the country. The platform uses advanced artificial intelligence and machine learning to provide personalised travel recommendations, price predictions, and real-time updates to travellers. ixigo's AI-powered features include flight price predictions, live train running status, PNR status check, seat availability prediction and a highly accurate flight delay predictor. ixigo is committed to making travel affordable, seamless and enjoyable for every Indian traveller — from budget backpackers to business class explorers.
